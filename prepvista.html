Frequently Asked

Questions Institutions Usually Ask.

Do students need training or technical skills to use the platform?
+
No. PrepVista works on any smartphone browser — no app installation required. Students sign up in under 2 minutes, upload their resume with one tap, and start their first mock interview immediately. The interface is simpler than most social media apps. If your students can use WhatsApp, they can use PrepVista. We also send a 3-minute onboarding video with the student invitation email.
Will students actually use it regularly?
+
Three things drive consistent student usage: fear, convenience, and visible results. Your students are already afraid of interviews — that is the most powerful motivator. PrepVista is available on their phones at any hour — that is convenience. And the moment a student sees their specific, actionable score after their first session, they return. Your TPO also has a live usage dashboard — if a student is not practising, you know immediately and can intervene. We also support making a minimum number of sessions mandatory as a placement registration condition.
How much work does implementation require from our IT team?
+
None. PrepVista is entirely cloud-based. We handle setup, configuration, and deployment. You need zero server infrastructure, zero software installation, and zero technical expertise on your end. The TPO uploads a CSV of student emails — that is the most technical task required from your college. Everything else is handled by our team.
Can each department track students separately?
+
Yes. The platform supports Department, Year, Batch, and Section organisation. HODs see only their department's data. The Placement Officer sees institution-wide analytics. The Principal sees executive summaries. Role-based access is configured during setup. There is also a complete audit trail of every admin action — every grant or revoke is logged with a timestamp.
Can the platform carry our college's branding?
+
Yes. The platform supports institutional branding — your college name and identity are applied to the student interface. Students see a platform that feels like it belongs to your college, not a third-party tool. This matters for student adoption and for how the platform appears in NAAC documentation as a college-led initiative.
How fast can we be fully operational?
+
Most institutions are fully operational within 7–14 days. A pilot cohort — one department, one batch — can begin within 48 hours of your confirmation. We provide dedicated onboarding support throughout the setup period. There is no waiting list, no complex procurement process, and no minimum commitment for a pilot.
Is student data secure and kept private?
+
Yes. Student data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Institutional data is never shared with third parties — including recruiters — without explicit permission. Students' interview recordings and reports are accessible only to the student and the college admin. A detailed data handling policy is available on request before any commitment.
Can placement officers monitor individual student progress?
+
Yes. The TPO dashboard shows every student's readiness score, total sessions completed, skill gap analysis, score progression over time, and current interview-ready status. You can filter by department, year, or batch. The bottom 20% of performers are automatically flagged for intervention. Reports can be exported as CSV for sharing with HODs or for NAAC submissions.
We already have a training department. Do we still need this?
+
PrepVista does not replace your training department — it multiplies their impact. Think of it this way: your TPO is the coach, PrepVista is the practice ground. A cricket coach is essential, but the team also needs a nets facility to practice individually, every day, with data on every ball they face. Your placement cell handles strategy, corporate relationships, and human mentoring. PrepVista handles the 500-student-scale practice problem that no human team can solve alone.
Is there a pilot option before full commitment?
+
Yes — and we strongly encourage it. We propose starting with a 2-week free pilot: one department, 20–30 students, full admin access for your TPO. No payment. No contract. You see real data from your own students before making any decision. If the data supports a partnership, we formalise it. If it does not, you have invested nothing except 30 minutes of setup time.
